Mission Statement

Correll Consulting evaluates client's websites to ensure full compliance with modern accessibility standards. We test, and if need be modify, every aspect of a website so that you can be confident in your website's construction. It is our goal to ensure that every website conforms to the unique needs of it's individual users, no matter their background.

What We Do

Accessibility Reports

Our specialists will review your website in it's entirety and provide a detailed report of all accessibility violations present. You will receive both a physical and digital copy of the report. The report will cover every facet of Accessibility and will assess how your website performs.

Website Modification

Our programmers will personally fix all relevant issues with your website to ensure it meets accessibility standards. In addition, they will train your employees in managing and updating it. An Accessibility Report must be obtained prior to requesting this service.

Annual/Biannual Maintenance

Our programmers will routinely evaluate and update your website to ensure that it remains compliant. We will fix any issues created and update any outdated formats. A Website Modification service must be provided prior to requesting this service.

Why Correll Consulting?

We at Correll Consulting strive to provide you with the best customer service on the market. We offer personalized support to ensure that you are in the know every step of the way. We guarantee that your website will be more accessible or your money back!
