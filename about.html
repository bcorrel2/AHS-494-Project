About Us

Ben Correll

Ben is the founder and CEO of Correll Consulting. A Junior in Computer Science and Linguistics at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign, Ben's mission is to ensure the internet is a usable and accessible place for everyone. Ben specializes in accessible tech, as well as natural language processing. When he is not busily typing away at a keyboard, Ben enjoys napping, eating and taking long walks through the park.
